Understanding You Are A Failure

One of first things you must do, is acknowledge that you are a failure. You must come to terms with the fact that you did not succeed at your task or being a father, or husband for that matter. So, first things first. Say it with me, "I am a failure!" "I am not a good Father!" "It's okay that my wife and kids hate me!" "It is okay that I am not respected. Men dont deserve respect. Men are meant to provide and nothing else. Its my own fault that my wife isnt attracted to me mentally or physically. I should have been a better man. Its my fault my children dont respecxt me. I should have been a better father." Now this last one may be difficult to accept, but you still need to to say it and accept it. "I accept my wife and children dont and wont love or respect me because I am worthless and a failure."

See. that wasn't so hard, now was it.
